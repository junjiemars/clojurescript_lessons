Owl options
Excluded URLs or patterns
To disable Owl on a site, use
[<scheme>://]<host-pattern>[:<port>] to match all hostnames, A leading "." is interpreted as a "*.", eg: "*foo.com", "https://x.*.y.z:99"; Or

[<scheme>://]<ip-literal>[:<port>] to match IP address literals, eg: "127.0.0.1", "[0:0::1]", "http://[::1]:99"; Or

<ip-literal>/<prefix-length-in-bits> to match an IP literal within the given range, eg: "192.168.1.1/16", "fefe:13::abc/33"
To view all available shortcuts, type ? to show the Owl help dialog.
